A Little About the Band

Formed in Boston in 1976, the Cars consisted of Ric Ocasek, Benjamin Orr, Elliot Easton, David Robinson, and Greg Hawkes, all of whom had been in and out of multiple bands throughout the 1970s. Their sound was descibed as new age, synthrock, or power pop. The American rock band the Cars number of albums includes seven studio albums, eight compilation albums, four video albums and 26 singles.

Band Members

Ocasek and Orr shared lead vocals, and Ocasek was the band's principal songwriter and leader.

  • Ric Ocasek (rhythm guitar)
  • Benjamin Orr (bass guitar),
  • Elliot Easton (lead guitar)
  • Greg Hawkes (keyboards)
  • David Robinson (drums)
